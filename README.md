In educational institutions, exam timetables are typically distributed in the form of 
printed notices or PDF documents. These formats are often complex, containing 
multiple branches, dates, and subjects arranged in grid structures, making it 
difficult for students to quickly extract relevant information. As a result, students 
may miss exams, misunderstand schedules, or spend unnecessary time searching 
for their specific details.
With the advancement of web technologies, there is a need for intelligent systems 
that can automate timetable interpretation and provide personalized information 
to users. The Smart Exam Reminder system addresses this need by combining 
OCR-based text extraction, structured data processing, and real-time notifications 
into a single platform.
This system enables administrators to upload exam timetables and allows students 
to view only the exams relevant to them. It enhances accessibility, improves time 
management, and reduces dependency on manual tracking. The inclusion of 
features like countdown timers and reminder notifications further ensures that 
students stay informed and prepared.
